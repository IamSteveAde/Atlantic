Crude Oil Refining

Atlantic operates a 2,000 barrels-per-day modular refinery engineered for flexibility, speed, and scalability. This facility refines crude into vital products including Automotive Gas Oil (AGO), Kerosene, and Naphtha.

With a strategic supply agreement in progress with the Nigerian Petroleum Development Company (NPDC) and other international partners, we ensure a steady feedstock flow and reliable downstream distribution.

Jetty & SBM Operations

Located along the coastline in the Brass Free Trade Zone, our commercial jetty supports vessels up to 25,000 DWT. This facility ensures efficient loading and unloading of refined products, reducing logistics bottlenecks in the region.

In Phase II, we plan to expand into Single Buoy Mooring (SBM) operations, establishing Atlantic as a logistics and transshipment hub for petroleum products across West Africa.

Power Generation

Our embedded 2MW power plant, delivered by Caterpillar, supplies clean and consistent energy to our refinery and the local Brass community. We are committed to providing free electricity to over 1,000 homes as part of our social impact mission.

Future plans include scaling the plant up to 20MW to power other industries within the Atlantic Energy Park and the surrounding Free Trade Zone.
